## Tips for Presentation

- **Practice the flow**: Go through the demo at least once before the presentation

- **Focus on user benefits**: Emphasize how each feature helps users accomplish their goals
- **Address questions confidently**: If you don't know something, it's okay to say "That's a great question - let me note that for future consideration"
- **Show enthusiasm**: Your passion for the design will come through
- **Pace yourself**: Don't rush - give time for the audience to absorb what they're seeing
